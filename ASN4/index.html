Key Insight

TF-IDF with cosine similarity effectively captures lexical similarity but may not always distinguish semantic nuances like negation. The high similarity between "I love football" and "I do not love football" demonstrates this limitation, while the comparison of sports-related sentences shows strong performance for semantically similar content.
